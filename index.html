Hello, and welcome to The Quill.

"Man is least himself when he talks in his own person. Give him a mask, and he will tell you the truth." -Oscar Wilde


If we are least ourselves when we speak as ourselves, then let us be your mask. Here, we provide the opportunity to speak through an intermediary-- or a mask-- in order to tell a truth. A truth that might be too difficult to tell as ourselves, or one that we do not feel we are best equipped to deliver. Even, perhaps, simply one that we wish to e received in a unique and warm way.

Here you will find a service unlike any other.
